Once upon a time, there was a kind and beautiful girl named Cinderella. She lived with her wicked stepmother and two mean stepsisters who made her do all the housework. They were cruel to her, but Cinderella always stayed kind and hopeful.

One day, the King invited everyone to a royal ball so the Prince could find a bride. Cinderella was excited to go, but her stepmother wouldn't let her. Instead, she had to help her stepsisters get ready for the ball while she stayed at home feeling sad.

But then, a fairy godmother appeared! With a wave of her magic wand, she turned Cinderella's rags into a beautiful gown and her old shoes into glass slippers. She even turned a pumpkin into a golden carriage! The fairy godmother warned Cinderella that the magic would only last until midnight.

At the ball, the Prince was enchanted by Cinderella and they danced all night. But at midnight, Cinderella remembered the fairy godmother's warning and ran out of the palace, leaving behind one glass slipper.

The Prince searched the kingdom for the girl whose foot fit the slipper. When he found Cinderella, it was a perfect fit! They got married and lived happily ever after.
